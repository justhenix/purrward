# Purrward Watercolor Asset Inventory Spec (Optimized)

**Budget**: ~17 assets total. Designed for a solo artist in a 10-day sprint (40-50 hours total).
**Theme**: Cozy watercolor, soft pastel edges, paper texture, hand-drawn lines.

---

## 1. Cat Avatars (6 assets)

_Resolution: 1024x1024 PNG (or SVG), transparent background. Sitting pose._

- High-res for tablet/desktop scaling and crisp rendering on high-DPI (Retina) mobile screens.
- Aligned anchor points for clothes/accessories overlay.

- **Base Cat Bodies (3 assets)**:
  - `cat_orange_tabby.png`
  - `cat_tuxedo.png` (Black & White)
  - `cat_grey.png` (Solid Grey)
- **Face/State Overlays (3 assets)**:
  - `face_happy.png` (Closed eyes, smiling curve)
  - `face_sad.png` (Droopy eyes, slight tongue blep)
  - `face_sleepy.png` (Closed horizontal line eyes, Zzz doodles)

## 2. Accessories & Outfits (4 assets)

_Resolution: 1024x1024 PNG, transparent background. Matches avatar scaling._

- **Headwear (2 assets)**:
  - `acc_flower_crown.png`
  - `acc_rain_hat.png` (Cozy yellow bucket hat)
- **Neckwear & Face (2 assets)**:
  - `acc_bandanna.png`
  - `acc_glasses.png` (Round wire-rimmed glasses)

## 3. Backgrounds (2 assets)

_Resolution: 1125x2436 PNG (Standard 19.5:9 high-DPI mobile aspect ratio)._

- Tailored for full-screen mobile backgrounds without stretching/pixelation.

- `bg_home.png`: Cozy living room corner with a warm fireplace, a bookshelf, and a soft watercolor wash border.
- `bg_park.png`: Rolling green hills under a soft blue sky, with floating cherry blossom petals and a cardboard box.

## 4. UI Elements & Accents (5 assets)

_Used for habit icons, card backdrops, and Polaroid scrapbook._

- **Watercolor Splotches / Blobs (3 assets)**:
  - _Resolution: 256x256 PNG or SVG._
  - `ui_splotch_pink.png`
  - `ui_splotch_yellow.png`
  - `ui_splotch_green.png`
- **Polaroid Frame (1 asset)**:
  - _Resolution: 1200x1600 PNG or SVG (aspect ratio 3:4 or 4:5)._
  - `ui_polaroid.png`: High-res overlay to wrap uploaded camera photos without blur.
- **Gacha Jar (1 asset)**:
  - _Resolution: 512x512 PNG or SVG._
  - `ui_gacha_jar.png`
